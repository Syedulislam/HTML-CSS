Welcome to Shikhbe Shobai!

Shikhbe Shobai (শিখবে সবাই) was founded by 11 like-minded, young and motivated individuals with a vision to take freelancing as a career path to 160 million Bangladeshis and to create a community of 10,000 successful freelancers within 2018. Our mission is to help People learn the on demand skills they need to achieve your full potential and establish a path towards a brighter Digital Bangladesh.
